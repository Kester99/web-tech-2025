The Golden Pond

The hedgehog led them through twisty vines. The air grew warmer, like they had entered a new world.

They reached a golden pond, glowing like sunlight. The water shimmered and showed stars from a sky they didn’t know.

"It’s so pretty," Amaia whispered, kneeling by the edge.

In the middle stood a stone pedestal with a glowing golden key on top.

As Iona reached for it, the pond shook. A deep voice said:

"Only those who solve the puzzle may take the key..."

Glowing shapes appeared on the water. "It’s a puzzle," Amaia said. "Let’s solve it."
