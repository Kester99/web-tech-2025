The Secret Garden of Iona & Amaia

Iona and Amaia had always wondered about the ivy-covered gate at the edge of the forest.

One evening, a soft breeze pushed it open, revealing a hidden enchanted garden. As they stepped inside, the air shimmered, and the world around them changed.

In the distance, a glowing butterfly fluttered near a silver tree, while a curious hedgehog watched them from beneath a twisted archway.
